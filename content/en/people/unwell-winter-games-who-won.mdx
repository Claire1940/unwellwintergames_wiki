## Challenge Breakdown: The Seven-Layer Gauntlet

The final day of the competition featured a gauntlet of seven events. Each event tested a different set of "skills," ranging from athletic prowess to a complete lack of dignity. Understanding the individual event winners is crucial to seeing how the final score was tallied.

### 1. Undress to Redress
In this challenge, one competitor from each team had to strip down to their underwear and change into a full set of winter clothes while standing on a tree. 
*   **Winner:** Dylan (Blue Team)
*   **Prize:** $5,000

### 2. Snowshoe Shuffle
Two members from each team were tied together at the ankles and tasked with racing across a snow-covered finish line.
*   **Winner:** Blue Team (Anna and Nico)
*   **Prize:** $5,000

### 3. Flip Cup
A classic collegiate drinking game brought to the professional stage. This was a race to see which team could flip all their cups first.
*   **Winner:** Purple Team (Juliet)
*   **Prize:** $5,000

### 4. Downhill Ski
A slalom-style race from the top of the hill to the bottom. Speed and precision were required to navigate the gates.
*   **Winner:** A.O. (Blue Team)
*   **Prize:** $5,000

### 5. Best Wipeout
Judged by a panel, this event rewarded the player who could perform the most entertaining and dramatic fall on the slopes.
*   **Winner:** Louie (Blue Team)
*   **Prize:** Point awarded to Blue Team

### 6. Makeout Challenge
Teams were judged on the passion, choreography, and "freakiness" of their kissing performances.
*   **Winner:** Demi and Brett (Purple Team)
*   **Prize:** Point awarded to Purple Team

### 7. The Roast Off
The final and most important event. Worth three points, this challenge required competitors to verbally dismantle the opposing team.
*   **Winner:** Huda (Purple Team)
*   **Prize:** 3 Points (Secured the team win)
