The climax of the **unwell winter games episode guide** is undoubtedly the Roast Off between Cameron and Huda. > **⚠️ Warning:** The Roast Off is highly personal. Contestants often bring up "childhood trauma" and "insecurities" to get a rise out of their opponents.
