## The "Weakest Link" Strategy

The core of the **unwell winter games eliminated contestants** selection process is the "Weakest Link" vote. Teams are required to sit together and publicly nominate the person they feel contributes the least. 

1.  **Peer Nomination:** Teams must reach a consensus or the opposing team chooses for them.
2.  **The Twist:** Being voted the weakest link doesn't always mean instant elimination; it often leads to a "Redemption Duel."
3.  **Replacements:** Eliminated players are frequently replaced by "Surprise Guests" like Dylan or Demi to keep the house numbers consistent.

This format encourages players to either be the most athletic or the most socially integrated. As seen with Hie, being well-liked can lead to someone else (like Andrew) taking your place in a dangerous elimination scenario.
