## Key Challenges and Gameplay Mechanics

The Unwell Winter Games are divided into two parts: a team-based challenge and an individual elimination round. Success in these events often depends on the contestants' ability to adapt to the harsh 2026 winter conditions.

1.  **Snowmo Cross:** A time-trial snowmobile race where partners must match follower counts to contestants. Every error adds a 5-second penalty.
2.  **Beer Pong Duel:** A high-stakes elimination game. In Episode 2, Louis Russell defeated Andrew in a head-to-head match, securing his spot and a $10,000 cash prize.
3.  **Launch Me, Daddy:** A tubing competition where contestants are launched down a hill toward a bullseye. The goal is to land as close to the center as possible.
