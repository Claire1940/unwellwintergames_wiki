## Challenge Breakdown: Stage 1 and Stage 2

The challenges in the unwell wintergames are divided into physical trials and "mental/social" tests. Each stage offers specific advantages, such as the ability to choose the turn order for the next round or earning individual cash prizes that can be used to influence other competitors.

### Stage 1: Snowmo Frost
This snowmobile time trial required pairs to navigate a mountain course while retrieving a sack containing follower count tokens. Players had to match these tokens to the correct creators, with each error adding a five-second penalty to their total time. This tested both coordination and the players' knowledge of their peers' social standing.

## FAQ

**Q: How are the teams decided in the unwell wintergames?**

A: The winners of the initial time trial become captains. In 2026, Anna Delvey and Alyssa Violet were the captains. They then pick players, but they must pick for the *opposing* team, creating a roster filled with strategic friction.

**Q: What happens to the "Weakest Link" in the competition?**

A: Each team must nominate a Weakest Link through a 15-minute deliberation. These individuals typically face a head-to-head elimination challenge, such as the Beer Pong duel seen in Episode 2.

**Q: Is there a grand prize for the winner of the Unwell Wintergames?**

A: While the final grand prize details are often kept under wraps, individual challenges offer significant rewards, including $10,000 cash prizes for specific event winners like Nico and Louis.

**Q: Can production members join the cast?**

A: Yes, as demonstrated by Andrew in the 2026 season, production members can be drafted into the competition if a scheduled competitor misses their flight or is otherwise unable to participate.
