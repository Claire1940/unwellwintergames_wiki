## Launch Me, Daddy: The Tubing Bullseye

The physical half of the team challenge, "Launch Me, Daddy," required contestants to slide down a massive snow hill in a tube, aiming for a bullseye at the bottom. The Purple Team used their advantage to force the Blue Team to go first, allowing them to observe the physics of the track and adjust their speed and trajectory.

This challenge also featured an individual prize of **$10,000** for the person who landed closest to the center. While many struggled to even stay on the track, Nico (the "Daddy" of the Purple Team) delivered a near-perfect run.

1. **Cameron (Blue):** Landed in the bullseye but was off-center.
2. **Juliet (Purple):** Lost control and veered off the track.
3. **Nico (Purple):** Secured the center spot with a massive launch.
4. **Huda (Purple):** Maintained team momentum with a solid landing.
5. **Hannah (Blue):** Failed to reach the bullseye, which would later prove fatal for her game.

The Purple Team won the overall challenge, securing safety for the entire group. Nico walked away with the $10,000, promising to take his "baddies" out for a celebratory night.
