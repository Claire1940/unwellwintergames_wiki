## FAQ

**Q: What is the largest single prize in the Unwell Winter Games?**

A: The largest prize mentioned in the 2026 season is the $250,000 team stake. However, this requires a team to win eight consecutive rounds without a single loss. Individual prizes are typically set at $10,000 per major challenge.

**Q: Do players get to keep the money if they are eliminated later?**

A: Yes, once a player wins an individual cash prize, such as the $10,000 from the Beer Pong or Tubing challenges, that money is theirs to keep, regardless of their eventual standing in the competition.

**Q: Are there any non-cash unwell winter games prizes?**

A: Absolutely. Non-cash prizes include luxury dinners, immunity from elimination, and the "Butler Reward," where the winning team receives personal service from the losing team for an entire evening.

**Q: How are the winners of the trivia challenges determined?**

A: Winners are determined by a "majority vote" system. The production team asks the entire house a series of spicy questions, and the "correct" answer is whoever received the most votes from their fellow competitors, often leading to significant drama and revealed secrets.
