### Launch Me, Daddy (Extreme Tubing)
The "Launch Me, Daddy" challenge is a two-part event that tests both the mind and the body. Part one consists of a trivia contest where teams answer personal (and often scandalous) questions about their fellow competitors. Winning the trivia round grants a strategic advantage in the second part: the tubing launch.

In the tubing portion, players are launched down a steep incline in giant tubes, aiming for a bullseye target at the bottom. 

**Tubing Physics and Tactics:**
*   **Weight Distribution:** Keeping your center of gravity low and your "butt up" prevents the tube from catching unnecessary friction.
*   **The Bump Mechanic:** The team going second has the opportunity to physically bump the opposing team out of the bullseye. While physical violence is technically prohibited, aggressive "nudging" is highly encouraged.
*   **Team Coordination:** Having "spotters" at the top of the hill to push the tube at the correct angle is vital for hitting the target.
