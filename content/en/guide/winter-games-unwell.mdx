## The Seven-Layer Gauntlet

By the final day of the **winter games unwell**, the pressure reached a breaking point. The "Seven-Layer Gauntlet" was introduced, a series of rapid-fire challenges designed to test everything from dignity to lung capacity. Each layer was worth one point, except for the final "Roast Off," which was worth a massive three points.

1.  **Undress to Redress:** A test of speed and dexterity where Dylan Mulvaney secured a win for the Blue Team.
2.  **Snowshoe Shuffle:** A cooperative race where Anna Delvey and her partner took the point.
3.  **Flip Cup:** A classic frat-style showdown won by Juliet for the Purple Team.
4.  **Downhill Ski:** A slalom race where Al secured a victory for Blue.
5.  **Best Wipeout:** A judged event where Louis Russell’s dramatic fall beat out Saraya’s professional wrestling-inspired tumble.
6.  **Makeout Challenge:** A controversial round judged on "passion and choreography," won by Demi and her husband (a surprise guest).
