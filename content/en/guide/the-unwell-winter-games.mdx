The 2026 season introduced the "Seven-Layer Gauntlet," a series of rapid-fire events designed to strip away the players' dignity while rewarding those with the most versatile skill sets. 5.  **Best Wipeout:** A judged event where the most entertaining fall earns the point.
6.  **Makeout Challenge:** A performance-based event judged on passion and choreography.
