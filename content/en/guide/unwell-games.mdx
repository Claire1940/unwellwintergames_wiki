## Core Challenges and Mechanics

The challenges in the **unwell games** are designed to be "deeply unwell," blending traditional winter sports with psychological hurdles. Success in these events requires a mix of athletic ability and "street smarts."

### 1. Snowmo Frost (Snowmobile Trials)
The opening event required pairs to navigate a snowmobile course while retrieving a sack of tokens. These tokens contained the follower counts of every competitor. The "Passenger Princess" had to correctly match the counts to the creators. Every error added five seconds to the team's final time. This challenge emphasized that in the world of the **unwell games**, your social stats are literally your currency.

### 2. Trivia: Social Warfare
The trivia rounds are notoriously personal. Questions focus on the "red flags" and scandalous histories of the participants. For example, round winners in 2026 correctly identified:
*   Which competitor slid into Selena Gomez's DMs without a reply.
*   Who carries four separate phones as a "red flag."
*   Sexual kinks and "hall passes" involving mainstream celebrities.

### 3. Launch Me, Daddy (Tubing)
This physical challenge involves launching competitors down a steep hill in giant tubes with the goal of landing in a distant bullseye. The winning team gains a significant advantage in the following elimination round. In 2026, Nico Emanuel emerged as a frontrunner in this event, securing a $10,000 cash prize for his precision.

### 4. Beer Pong Duel
When the competition gets down to the "Weakest Link," players may face off in high-stakes duels. The 2026 beer pong duel between Louis Russell and the production-assistant-turned-player Andrew was a highlight. Despite Andrew's "heart," Louis’s international experience secured him the stay and the cash prize.
