## Game Rules and Challenges

The competition is structured around "Winter Games" that test both physical endurance and social IQ. The first major challenge, **Snowmo Frost**, set the tone for the season. This snowmobile time trial required pairs to navigate a course while performing a "Passenger Princess" task: retrieving a sack of tokens and correctly identifying the follower counts of their fellow competitors.

### Challenge Mechanics: Snowmo Frost
1. **The Drive:** One partner drives the snowmobile at full throttle.
2. **The Grab:** The passenger must snatch a sack containing numerical tokens.
3. **The Match:** Pairs must match the correct follower count to the correct creator.
4. **The Penalty:** Every incorrect match adds a **five-second penalty** to the total time.

The stakes in 2026 are higher than ever, with a **$10,000 cash prize** often attached to specific challenge wins. However, the ultimate goal is survival and avoiding the "Weakest Link" designation.
