## Episode 2 Recap: The Butter Block and "Launch Me, Daddy"

Episode 2 centers on the fallout of the first elimination vote. The teams were forced to nominate their own "weakest links" to face off in a duel. Louie (Blue Team) and Harie (Purple Team) were the targets, but the "Unwell Winter Games" threw a curveball that shattered the illusion of unscripted reality.

### The Producer Plant: Andrew Veter
In a highly staged sequence, a production crew member named Andrew Veter (known for his mullet and single dangling earring) "sacrificed" his job to take Harie’s place in the elimination. While presented as a romantic gesture, it was clear that Andrew was an embedded actor designed to control the narrative. 

The elimination challenge involved a game of Beer Pong to win **$10,000 cash** hidden inside a massive block of Carrie Gold butter. Louie defeated Andrew, securing the cash and his spot in the house. However, in a bizarre turn of events, Andrew didn't leave; he simply put on an apron and became the house’s in-house bartender.

The second major challenge involved two parts: a deep-secret trivia round and a high-speed tubing competition. The trivia revealed scandalous details, such as Louie possessing four active cell phones and Dakota’s failed attempts to message A-list celebrities.
